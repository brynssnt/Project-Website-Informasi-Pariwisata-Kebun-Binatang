Park Visit Advisory

The safety and well-being of our guests, staff and animals are our priority and we have put in place the necessary Safe

Management Measures, so everyone can enjoy a meaningful wildlife experience in a safe and healthy environment.

Please expect some changes to our parks and be guided by the measures. While not all activities will be immediately

available, there is still much to enjoy in the wide open spaces of our parks with our animal family.

"Feed the Giraffe."

he Feeding Station features a gated feeding area where visitors can purchase a small branch of acacia browse to feed a giraffe. Guests may also enjoy a separate, non-gated deck for free viewing and photography. A large hand-washing station is found behind the feeding area always wash after feeding a giraffe!

"Meet the Pandas"

Are you absolutely wild about pandas? How would you like to tour Bamboo Forest before the zoo opens and watch the Giant Pandas Wang Wang and Fu Ni bring in the new day with plenty of exploring around their exhibit. Finish off your experience with morning tea at our cafe and meet our other amazing animals on a guided tour around the rest of kebun binatang brisurs.

"Play With Penguin"

Go behind the scenes of our Cold Water Quest gallery for an up-close-and-personal penguin encounter. You'll learn all about how we care for them, as well as our conservation initiatives all of which are supported by your ticket dollars and participation in this 30-minute program.
